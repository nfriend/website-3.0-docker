Since chances are high you don't own a Leap Motion controller, I put together a quick video to demonstrate how my digital Theremin operates:


Making noise

The Leap Motion controller provides detailed 3D spatial information for all hands within its detection radius. In addition, it reports the position and angle of each palm relative to the ground. With this many data points, I was able to give each modifier its own axis, giving the user full control over the instrument's output:

Pitch:
Hand #1, Y axis
Volume:
Hand #1, Z axis
Tone:
Hand #1, palm rotation
Vibrato speed:
Hand #2, Y axis
Vibrato intensity:
Hand #2, Z axis
